Security Standards

Encryption & Protection

We use 256-bit AES encryption for all stored data and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. We host on private, regional cloud servers to ensure data sovereignty. Our "Recursive Command Center" uses isolated infrastructure to ensure your business data is never shared or leaked.

Compliance

We continuously test and harden infrastructure and follow best-practice operational security and incident response procedures. Our team maintains compliance with industry standards and regulations including HIPAA for healthcare clients.

We undergo regular third-party security audits and penetration testing to ensure the highest standards of protection. Detailed security reports are available upon request.
